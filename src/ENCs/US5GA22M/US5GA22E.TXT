For bascule bridges, whose spans do not open to a full upright or vertical position, unlimited vertical clearance is not available for the entire charted horizontal clearance.


High water clearances, for bridges and overheard calbles, north of S.S. Railroad Bascule Bridge, refer to a water plane established by a discharge of 175.56 cubic meters per second / 62,000 cubic feet per second at New Savannah Bluff Dam. Low water clearances north of the bridge refer to a discharge of 172.73 cubic meters per second / 6,100 cubic feet per second. Clearances at the bridge and to the south refer to Mean High Water
