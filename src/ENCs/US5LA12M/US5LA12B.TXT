INTRACOASTAL WATERWAY PROJECT DEPTHS:
The project depth is 3.6 meters/12 feet Carrabelle, FL to Brownsville, TX.
Consult the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers for controlling depths and U.S. Coast Guard Local
Notice to Mariners for other navigation hazards or restrictions.
DISTANCES:
Mileage distances shown along the waterway are in Statute Miles, based on zero Lock, LA. 
Tables for converting Statute Miles to International Nautical Miles are given in U.S. Coast Pilot 5.
