PROJECT DEPTHS: The project depth is 1.8 meters/6 feet Manasquan Inlet, NJ to Ottens Harbor, NJ;
3.0 meters/10 feet Ottens Harbor, NJ to Richardson Channel,
NJ; 3.6 meters/12 feet Richardson Channel, NJ to Cape May
Inlet, NJ.
Consult the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers for controlling depths and U.S. Coast Guard Local Notice to Mariners
for other navigation hazards or restrictions.
Uncharted shoals may exist in areas which have
not been recently surveyed. Please reported shoals
and obstructions at:
http://nauticalcharts.noaa.gov/staff/contact.htm


DISTANCES: The general location of the Waterway is indicated
by a magenta line. Mariners are advised to follow
the aids to navigation and avoid charted shoals
and obstructions.
Mileage distances shown along the Waterway
are in Statute Miles southward from Manasquan
Inlet Entrance at 40°06´N Latitude, 74°02´W Longi-
tude and are indicated thus:
One Statute Mile equals 0.87 Nautical Miles / 1.6 Kilometers,
Courses are TRUE and must be CORRECTED
for any variation and compass deviation.
